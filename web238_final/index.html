About Us

G's Frozen Custard

We are a locally owned business and have been serving up smiles since 1991. Located in central Topeka, we are within 10 minutes from most locations in the capital city. A hidden treat to some, we are famous in our own minds and take pride in making fresh batches of frozen custard on a daily basis. We are known for our Famous Brown Bread Frozen Custard, but over time our menu has expanded and we now offer between 10 - 15 unique frozen custard flavors on a daily basis. We also offer a no sugar added frozen yogurt product that taste remarkable. 2016 / 2017 / 2018 / 2019 / 2020 / 2021 / 2022 / 2023 / 2024 Winner of Best of Topeka, 1st Place - Frozen Custard / Ice Cream / Frozen Yogurt Category. Thank you Topeka for supporting local and small businesses like ourselves.
