Donate

What does my donation help with?

$35 pays for spay/neuter surgery, rabies vaccine, and ear tip of one free-roaming/feral cat.

$120 pays for spay/neuter of one dog (depending upon sex/weight of dog)

$50-60 pays for spay/neuter of one cat (depending upon sex of cat)

However money isnt the only thing you can donate. We also accept:

  • Bleach
  • 70% Alcohol
  • Paper Towels
  • Toilet Tissues
  • Dawn Dish Soap
  • Cat Litter
  • Cat Food (Dry and Wet)
  • Dog Food (Dry and Wet)
  • Treats (Dogs and Cats)
  • Cotton Balls
  • Cotten Swabs
