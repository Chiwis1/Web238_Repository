Pet Grooming

Hello, my name us Terry

I have been working with animals for over 30 years. I owned and operated three pet supply stores with grooming salons in California. I have been a professional pet groomer for over 20 years. I am a certified veterinary assistant, and lifelong animal advocate.

I have been a crazy cat lady all my life, so it's only natural that they are my specialty. I welcome hard to groom cats, scaredy cats, matted diva cats, you name it! I groom all breeds of dogs, too. (I don't discriminate!) I am a little different from most groomers. Instead of dropping them off for the day, you are welcomed to watch. it takes me about an hour to groom most pets.
